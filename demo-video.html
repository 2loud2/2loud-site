What you just saw

A doorbell rings on TV. Gina — my wire-haired dachshund — hears it and goes on alert. I calm her down. We settle back in. Then a dog barks on TV. Gina loses it. The baby upstairs wakes up.

Two sounds. Two filters. That's what 2Loud catches.

With 2Loud on, the doorbell gets lowered before Gina reacts. The barking gets lowered before it escalates. The baby cry stays — because 2Loud only filters the categories you choose. The rest of your audio is untouched.

P.S. Ironically, through the process of making this video and website, Gina has been desensitized to doorbells and barking on TV. She no longer reacts. Nice job, Gina.
